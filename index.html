Pig Latin Translator

By: Pearl Lin

Pig Latin is a language game in which English words are altered, usually by moving consonants to the end of the word and adding "ay". It also happens to be a programming language. The rules of Pig Latin may vary slightly depending on where you look, but the guidelines below come from this source.

Pig Latin Rules

1. If a word starts with a consonant and a vowel, move the first letter of the word to the end and add "ay".


2. If a word starts with 2 consonants, move the consonants to the end of the word and add "ay".


3. If a word starts with a vowel, add "way" to the end of the word.
